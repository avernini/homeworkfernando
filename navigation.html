Step 2: Lexical Alignment (10 Min)

Everyday Street Prepositions

Study the core spatial markers below. Click on each card to hear correct native-level pronunciation, and practice repeating after the voice.

Preposition Audio Guide

Next to

Beside something; very close with nothing in between.

Beside

Right at the side of something. (Don't confuse with 'besides'!).

Between

In the middle of two distinct places or landmarks.

Go past

Walk beyond a building or location without entering it.

Across from

On the opposite side of the street or road.

On the corner of

At the exact point where two roads meet.

In front of

Directly ahead; facing the entrance of a place.

Behind

At the back of something; opposite of in front of.

Close to (Near)

Only a short distance away; not far.

💡 Teacher & Student Language Note:
• Beside = At the side of / next to. "The bakery is beside the bookstore."
• Besides = In addition to / except for. "Besides the park, what other sights should we visit?"
